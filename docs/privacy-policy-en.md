# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025**

## Introduction

SpeedMeter (the "App") is a speedometer application that uses GPS functionality to display real-time speed. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

### Location Information
- **GPS Location Data**: We access your device's GPS functionality to obtain location information for real-time speed measurement
- **Purpose**: Used solely for calculating and displaying your current speed

## How We Use Information

The collected location information is used only for the following purposes:

1. **Speed Calculation and Display**: Calculate your current speed from GPS data and display it in real-time
2. **Maximum Speed Recording**: Record and display the maximum speed during your session

## Data Storage and Sharing

### Data Storage
- All data is stored **only on your device**
- Location and speed data is **not transmitted to external servers**
- Location tracking stops when you close the App

### Third-Party Sharing
- The App **does not share** collected location information with any third parties
- We do not use advertising networks or analytics services
- We do not transmit data externally without user consent

## Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions:

1. **Access to Location** (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`)
   - Purpose: Speed measurement using GPS

2. **Prevent Screen from Sleeping** (`WAKE_LOCK`)
   - Purpose: Keep the screen on while traveling

## Data Security

- Location information is processed only in device memory
- Data is not transmitted over the network
- All data is deleted when the App is uninstalled

## Your Rights

### Managing Location Access
You can control location access at any time by:

1. Disabling location permission for this App in your device settings
2. Uninstalling the App

Disabling location access will prevent the App from functioning.

### Data Deletion
- All data is deleted when you uninstall the App
- Since the App does not store data in the cloud, data is completely removed when deleted from your device

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, we will update this page and change the "Last Updated" date. We recommend that you review this page periodically.
